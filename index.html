Explore the code and methodology on GitHub: Credit Card Fraud Detection Repository.

As one of 16 selected participants from my course, I traveled to La Palma, Canary Islands, to collect observational data for 5 nights using the high-resolution HERMES spectrometer at the Mercator Telescope. This data was pivotal in the research project I proposed with a teammate.

The project, titled "Constraining the Density of HBC+ in the ISM and DIB Correlations at High Reddening," investigates the formation of carbon fullerenes in the interstellar medium and explores correlations between diffuse interstellar bands (DIBs) and environmental parameters. I utilized data science techniques, including statistical modeling and error analysis, to process and interpret high-resolution spectral data.

Key findings from the study:

  • Improved upper limit on the column density of HBC+ (∼3.5 × 10¹² cm⁻²).
  • Introduced a data-driven method for telluric correction in the high-reddening regime.
  • Examined statistical correlations between DIBs and reddening.

About

They say curiosity leads to trouble – in my case, it led to the stars and back. From peering through telescopes to diving deep into datasets, my fascination with understanding the unknown has shaped every step of my journey. Now at KU Leuven, pursuing my Master's in Astronomy and Astrophysics, I'm channeling that same curiosity into unraveling the stories hidden in data.

What sets me apart is my unique blend of technical precision and creative thinking. My background in Electronics Engineering from BMSIT&M gave me a strong analytical foundation, while my artistic pursuits – from Bharatanatyam dance to culinary experimentation – bring a creative dimension to my problem-solving approach.

I believe the most innovative solutions emerge when we approach challenges from multiple angles. Whether I'm working with astronomical data or developing analytical models, I bring the same enthusiasm and methodical approach that first drew me to explore the cosmos

Living by the motto "Curiosity is the key to all our answers," I'm constantly seeking new ways to learn and grow. Beyond the numbers, I'm passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights that make a real difference. Let’s connect and explore the possibilities together!
